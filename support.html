What to Include When Reporting a Bug

To help us understand and fix the issue, please include:

  • Your iPhone model or Android device model.
  • Your iOS or Android version.
  • Your SalahGuard app version.
  • A clear description of the problem.
  • The steps you followed before the issue happened.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if possible.

Family Controls Issues

If you report a Family Controls or app blocking issue, please also mention whether Family Controls permission was granted, whether apps/categories were selected, and whether the problem happened during an active prayer period.

Response Time

We try to respond as soon as possible, but response times may vary.
