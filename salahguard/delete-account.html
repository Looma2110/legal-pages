SalahGuard Delete Account

Last updated: June 29, 2026

No Account Required

SalahGuard does not require a user account in the current release. Because there is no SalahGuard account, there is no cloud account to delete.

Delete Local App Data

If SalahGuard stores data locally on your device, you can remove local app data by deleting SalahGuard from your device. Depending on your device and platform, you may also be able to clear app data from system settings.

Subscriptions

Deleting the app does not automatically cancel an active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. You can manage or cancel subscriptions from your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.
